ASPEN MABATI

Aspen Mabati Limited is a local owned company registered in Kenya with a global vision of roofing the world one project at a time while providing excellent and efficient roofing solutions to the construction industry

The sole purpose of Aspen Mabati is to improve the roofing standards for private and commercial property developers by providing affordable quality roofing material in Kenya and East Africa. Our range of roofing materials include Aspen Eurotile, Aspen Box profile, Aspen Corrugated, Aspen Germantile, Aspen Versatile, Aspen Wavetile and other Roofing Accessories. We also provide Roof Installation services, Re-roofing and Roofing consultation
