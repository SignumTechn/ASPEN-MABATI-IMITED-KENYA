ASPEN CORRUGATED

Aspen Corrugated Sheets is made from high tensile steel that has a protective metal coating of 55% Aluminium, 43% Zinc and 1.6% Silicon, resulting in a product with an extremely long life, increased durability and a beautiful colour finish. It has a corrugated profile with 11 ribs and a pitch [distance between ribs] of 76.2mm. it is mainly used for roofing and walling in domestic applications.
Available in: -Tile red, Charcoal grey, Maroon, Sky blue, Jungle gree

Features and Benefits

• Lasts longer than a Zinc galvanized product of the same coated thickness.
• Offers up to 4 degrees’ cooler interiors.
• Has a shining beautiful silver finish.
• It is very easy to install
